Instructions for Uploading Your Translated Bitcoin Whitepaper

To upload your translated whitepaper, please follow these steps:

  • Carefully inspect your translation and save it as a PDF.
  • Upload the PDF file to virustotal.com and wait for the scan to complete.
  • Copy the link with the hash, which will look like this: https://www.virustotal.com/gui/file/b1674191a88ec5cdd733e4240a81803105dc412d6c6708d53ab94fc248f4f553.
  • Send me an email with:
    • - The VirusTotal link you copied before.
    • - Your carefully translated PDF file.
    • - Your credentials, including names and links to social profiles (LinkedIn, GitHub, Nostr, Twitter...).
    • - Note: not well known domains younger than 2 years will not be linked.

Thank you for contributing to the Bitcoin community!
