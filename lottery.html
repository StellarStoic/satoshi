Quick tutorial on how to play?

For playing this lottery you'll first need a Bitcoin Lightning address. And You can get one by installing a wallet like:

  • Strike: user@strike.me
  • Wallet of Satoshi: user@walletofsatoshi.com

Don't be scared if you're not familiar with Bitcoin Lightning ⚡. It is still Bitcoin — just on a faster highway where payments are nearly instant. So much about Bitcoin being slow, ha — as you often hear from bankers!

Once you have your Lightning address, top up some Bitcoin. (Both of these wallets have options to purchase Bitcoin directly inside the app.)

Now that you’ve got your Lightning address, enter it where it says “Enter your Lightning address” and lock it by clicking the yellow button.

You can now select options for your numbers: Single, Range, or Random. Choose the option that suits you best.

After choosing your numbers, it’s time to pay with Lightning by clicking the big yellow button below the total cost of the ticket price.

After clicking this button, a new window opens with the invoice. There you can either scan the QR code or copy the Lightning invoice string.

After a successful payment, the receipt in PDF format will be downloaded automatically.Make sure you save it! This receipt contains all the information about your ticket.

To make verifying your numbers easier, you can use the Ticket Verifier tool, where you'll see whether your ticket is a winner or not.

How are winning numbers generated?

Winners are determined using Bitcoin block hashes - completely transparent and verifiable!

Process:

  1. • At block 100 of each round, we take the block hash
  2. • Remove all letters (a-f), keeping only digits (0-9)
  3. • Take the last 8 digits as the winning number
  4. • Match your numbers from the right side

Example:Block 921700 hash
000000000000000000011203a39881ce5781e6905cd6456640495e09d09b5dcb
becomes 49509095 (winning number)

This ensures fairness - no one can manipulate Bitcoin block hashes!

How does matching work?

Numbers match from the RIGHT side only!

If winning number is 12345678:

  • • Tier 1 (8 matches) - 40% of the prize pool:
    12345678
  • • Tier 2 (7 matches) - 25% of the prize pool:
    92345678
  • • Tier 3 (6 matches) - 15% of the prize pool:
    99345678
  • • Tier 4 (5 matches) - 10% of the prize pool:
    99945678
  • • Tier 5 (4 matches) - 6% of the prize pool:
    99995678
  • • Tier 6 (3 matches) - 4% of the prize pool:
    99999678

Remember: Only consecutive matches from the right count!

Prize Splitting: If multiple players bet on the same winning number, the prize for that tier is split equally among all winning players.

When do Draws happen?

Every 100 Bitcoin blocks (approximately 16-17 hours)

Round Timeline:

  • Blocks 1-96: Betting window open
  • Blocks 97-99: Betting closed, system preparation
  • Block 100: Draw happens automatically

You can see the current block progress at the top of the page.

How do payouts work?

Instant automatic payments via Lightning Network!

  • • Winners are identified immediately after each draw
  • • Payments sent automatically to your Lightning Address
  • • You receive 86% of your prize tier amount
  • • 14% goes to operators for system maintenance
  • • Official receipt as PDF with proof provided for all transactions

No waiting, no manual claims needed!

Documentation: Every bet and payout includes an official receipt with transaction proof that you can download and keep for your records. You can always verify your PDF receipt at Ticket Verifier tool

What if there are no winners?

All bet money stays in the jackpot and rolls over to the next round, making it even bigger! This continues until someone wins.
